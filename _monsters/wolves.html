Wolf

Medium beast, unaligned
Armor Class 13 (natural armor)
Hit Points 11 (2d8 + 2)
Speed 40 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
12 (+1) 15 (+2) 12 (+1) 3 (-4) 12 (+1) 6 (-2)

Skills Perception +3, Stealth +4
Senses passive Perception 13
Languages --
Challenge 1/4 (50 xp)

Keen Hearing and Smell. The wolf has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

Pack Tactics. The wolf has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the wolf's allies is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally is not incapacitated.

Actions

Bite.

  • Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target
  • Hit: 7 (2d4 + 2) piercing damage
  • If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 11 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
